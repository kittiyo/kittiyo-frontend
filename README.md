# Photos Kittiyo 📸

Kittiyo? 👀

**Photos Kittiyo** is an open-source event photo delivery tool that lets people find their photos instantly using a selfie.

Upload all your event photos, share a link, and attendees can simply take a selfie to discover their pictures — no manual searching needed.

---

## ✨ How it works

1. Upload event photos
2. Share the link with attendees
3. Users take a selfie
4. Kittiyo finds and shows their photos instantly

---

## 🚀 Why Kittiyo?

- No more scrolling through hundreds of photos
- Fast face-based photo matching
- Simple link-based sharing
- Built for events, communities, and creators

---

## 🛠️ Open Source

Kittiyo is fully open-source and built for developers, event organizers, and makers.

Feel free to contribute, fork, and improve it 🚀

---

- College fests & events
- Weddings & parties
- Community meetups
